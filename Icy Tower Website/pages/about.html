Icy Tower's New Online Home: A Project by Gheorghe-Florin Hasna

In the digital age, the gaming community thrives on nostalgia and the love for classic games that have left an indelible mark on our hearts. One such game that has captured the hearts of players for generations is "Icy Tower". This fast-paced platformer game, originally created by Johan Peitz, has enjoyed a cult following for years. Now, thanks to the dedication and passion of a talented developer named Gheorghe-Florin Hasna, the Icy Tower community has a new online home.

Gheorghe-Florin Hasna embarked on a remarkable project to create a website that would serve as a central hub for Icy Tower enthusiasts. His vision was to offer a platform that not only celebrated the game's enduring appeal but also brought fans together. This project aimed to allow fans continue playing Icy Tower.
